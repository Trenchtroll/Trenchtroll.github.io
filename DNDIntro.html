Introduction

Greetings and well met traveller! Please come in and have a seat by the hearth and rest your weary feet. This website is help all people learn the basics of Dungeons and Dragons.

D&D is a tabletop role playing game, where a story is told by a Dungeon Master (DM). The players make characters and interact with the story told by the DM. The characters role dice to determine their succuss or failure in a given situation.

D&D is a fantasy world in which magic and monters exist. Dwarves and elves and orcs battle, Wizards cast spells while the Ranger hunts down his pray, and Clerics heal the wounded. The characters are heros of the world set out to fight evil, do good deeds and save the world from destruction.
